Lebron James

The GOAT of Basketball

Here's a time line of Lebron James life:(Last updated July 31,2024 )

  • 1984 - Born in Akron, Ohio
  • 2003-2004 - LeBron James, drafted first overall by Cleveland Cavaliers in 2003, began his rookie season at 18 and turned 19 with impressive performances, establishing him as a promising young NBA talent.
  • 2005 - LeBron was selected to his first NBA All-Star Game in 2005. He became the youngest player in NBA history to be selected for an All-Star game at 20 years old.
  • 2006 - LeBron led the Cavaliers to the playoffs for the first time in his career, averaging 30.8 points, 8.1 rebounds, and 5.8 assists in his playoff debut.
  • 2007 - In 2007, LeBron led the Cleveland Cavaliers to the NBA Finals, securing their first franchise title. LeBron's legendary performance against the Detroit Pistons in Game 5 secured their victory in six games.
  • 2010 - LeBron's Miami Heat move marked a new era in his career, as the team became title contenders under pressure and scrutiny to achieve their championship aspirations.
  • 2011 - LeBron James won his seventh consecutive NBA All-Star Game in 2011, showcasing his dominance among the league's elite players.
  • 2013 - LeBron James led Miami Heat to second consecutive NBA Championship, averaging 25.3 points, 10.9 rebounds, and 7 assists per game, earning MVP honors for the second consecutive year.
  • 2014 - LeBron James, Kyrie Irving, and Kevin Love formed the "Big Three" in Cleveland, transforming the Cavaliers into title contenders.
  • 2023 - LeBron James broke Kareem Abdul-Jabbar's record of 38,387 points in February 2023, becoming the NBA's all-time leading scorer.
